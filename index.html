Modern Slavery and Ecocide: Music and Poetry for Reflection

Songs on Modern Slavery and Ecocide

  • Mercy Mercy Me (The Ecology) by Marvin Gaye

    A soulful plea that expresses concern about environmental degradation, pollution, and the damage to nature caused by human greed and neglect.

  • Slave Driver by Bob Marley

    A reggae anthem denouncing oppression and exploitation, particularly focusing on the struggles faced by enslaved peoples.

Other songs dealing with similar themes of slavery and exploitation include Diamonds from Sierra Leone by Kanye West, which critiques the diamond trade and its impact on people and the environment.

Poems on Modern Slavery and Ecocide

  • The Cry of the Children by Elizabeth Barrett Browning

    A haunting poem that draws attention to the suffering of children in industrialized society and critiques societal neglect.
